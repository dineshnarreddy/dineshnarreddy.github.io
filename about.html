About AgriEdgeAI

AgriEdgeAI is Prajas Labs' first product — a PhD research-backed AI + IoT platform for precision agriculture. Developed in collaboration with the University of Portsmouth, with real-world pilots in Kadiri, Andhra Pradesh (India) and the Isle of Wight (UK).

Why we built it

Water stress, rising input costs, and climate variability make timely farm decisions critical. Existing tools are too expensive, too complex, or require constant internet. AgriEdgeAI works on a Raspberry Pi at the edge — no cloud dependency for real-time decisions.

Who it's for

Smallholder mango farmers in India, commercial tomato greenhouse operators in the UK, government extension workers, ICAR/NABARD partners, and CSR programmes focused on sustainable agriculture.
