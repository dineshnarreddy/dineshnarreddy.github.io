Research Partner — University of Portsmouth

AgriEdgeAI is developed as part of PhD research within the Faculty of Business and Law at the University of Portsmouth, United Kingdom — in collaboration with Prajas Labs Private Limited, India.

Research scope

The research investigates AI + IoT decision support for precision agriculture — covering irrigation optimisation, disease and pest detection, yield forecasting, and edge deployment on low-cost hardware (Raspberry Pi).

  • Pilot site (India): Kadiri, Andhra Pradesh — mango orchards
  • Pilot site (UK): Isle of Wight — commercial tomato greenhouse
  • Focus: explainable AI, offline-first, smallholder-appropriate
